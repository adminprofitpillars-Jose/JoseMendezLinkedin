## Engagement Tips for All Posts

- **Reply to every comment** on your posts within a few hours
- **Tag people** you met at events when relevant (with their permission)
- **Post between 8-10 AM** on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday for best visibility
- **Use 3-5 hashtags** per post (not more)
- **End with a question** to encourage engagement
- **Keep paragraphs short** -- LinkedIn rewards readability on mobile
- **Use line breaks** generously -- walls of text get scrolled past
