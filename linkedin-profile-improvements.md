## 8. Activity & Engagement Strategy

Consistency on LinkedIn matters more than perfection. Aim for:

- **2-3 posts per week** (learning insights, industry commentary, questions)
- **5-10 comments per day** on posts from marketers and data professionals you admire
- **Share your learning journey** -- people love following someone who's growing

**Post Ideas:**
- "Today I learned [concept] in my Google Data Analytics course. Here's why it matters for marketers..."
- Share an article about marketing trends and add your take
- "I'm attending [event] this week. Here's what I hope to learn..."
- Celebrate milestones (completed a course module, got a certification, attended a meetup)
