# Digital Marketers NYC Meetup -- April 14, 2026

## About the Event

**Organizer:** DigitalMarketersNYC
**Format:** Monthly marketing event and mixer for founders and marketers
**Link:** https://www.eventbrite.com/e/digital-marketers-nyc-meetup-tickets-1984896696451

DigitalMarketersNYC is a community hosting monthly events for founders and marketers. By bringing together startup founders and marketers, they create opportunities for effective networking and focus on resourceful, scrappy solutions to real-world problems. Stay informed on the latest marketing trends and effective strategies. Grow your network and join a collaborative community.

## Meeting Agenda

- **Lightning Talks** -- Two short presentations by members on a variety of digital marketing topics
- **Discussion** -- Share knowledge about the topics presented in the lightning talks and learn from other members in the community. You can participate or just listen
- **Guided Networking** -- Structured session to meet and interact with several people at each meeting, designed to help you connect meaningfully with other startup founders and marketing professionals
