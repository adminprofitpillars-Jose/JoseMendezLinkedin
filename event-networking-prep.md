# Event Networking Prep: Digital Marketers NYC Meetup

**Event:** Digital Marketers NYC Meetup
**Organizer:** Digital Marketers NYC
**Link:** https://www.eventbrite.com/e/digital-marketers-nyc-meetup-tickets-1984896696451
**Location:** NYC (Downtown Manhattan area)
**Format:** Lightning talks, group discussion, guided networking

---

## Before the Event

### Update Your LinkedIn Profile
- Make sure your headline, photo, and about section are polished (see linkedin-profile-improvements.md)
- Turn on "Open to Work" or "Open to Networking" if appropriate
- Update your profile URL to linkedin.com/in/josemendezp (already done)

### Prepare Your Introduction
Have a 30-second intro ready:

> "Hi, I'm Jose! I'm getting into digital marketing and currently working on my Google Data Analytics Certificate. I'm really interested in the data side of marketing -- understanding what drives results. I'm here to learn from people who are already in the field and find out what skills matter most day-to-day."

### Questions to Ask Other Attendees
1. "What's a tool or skill you wish you'd learned earlier in your marketing career?"
2. "How do you use data to inform your marketing decisions?"
3. "What marketing channels are you most excited about right now?"
4. "Are there any communities or resources you'd recommend for someone getting started?"
5. "What does a typical day look like in your role?"
6. "How did you break into digital marketing?"

### What to Bring
- Phone with LinkedIn app (QR code ready for quick connections)
- Business cards (if you have them; not required)
- A notebook or notes app for jotting down insights
- Positive attitude and genuine curiosity

---

## During the Event

### Networking Tips
- **Arrive early** -- smaller crowds make it easier to start conversations
- **Listen more than you talk** -- ask follow-up questions, show genuine interest
- **Take notes** -- jot down names, topics discussed, and any advice given
- **Connect on LinkedIn in real-time** -- "Let me add you right now!" feels more natural than "I'll find you later"
- **Attend the lightning talks** -- they give you easy conversation starters ("What did you think of that talk about X?")
- **Don't try to meet everyone** -- 3-5 meaningful conversations > 20 shallow ones

### During Guided Networking
- This is structured, so relax and follow the format
- Use your prepared intro
- Focus on finding 2-3 people you genuinely want to stay in touch with

---

## After the Event

### Within 24 Hours
1. **Send personalized connection requests** to everyone you spoke with:
   > "Hi [Name], it was great meeting you at the Digital Marketers NYC Meetup! I really enjoyed our conversation about [specific topic]. Would love to stay connected."

2. **Post about the event on LinkedIn** (see post templates in linkedin-post-templates.md)

3. **Follow up with key contacts** -- if someone offered advice or a resource, send a thank-you message

### Within the Week
4. **Write a short LinkedIn post** sharing 2-3 takeaways from the event
5. **Engage with other attendees' posts** about the event (like, comment, share)
6. **Join any group chats or communities** that were mentioned at the event

### Ongoing
7. **Attend regularly** -- the Digital Marketers NYC group meets monthly. Becoming a familiar face builds stronger relationships
8. **Consider volunteering** to give a lightning talk at a future meetup (even a short 5-minute talk on something from your Google Data Analytics course would be valuable)
